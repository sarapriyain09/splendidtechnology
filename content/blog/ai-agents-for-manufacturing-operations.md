AI agents are valuable when they remove repetitive coordination work, not when they replace core engineering decisions.

## High-value use cases

- Enquiry classification and assignment in CRM.
- Quote draft support using approved templates.
- Service ticket triage and escalation.
- Maintenance planning prompts based on condition alerts.
- Weekly summary reports for sales and operations managers.

## Guardrails that matter

- Use approved data sources only.
- Log all actions and approvals.
- Keep humans in final commercial and safety decisions.

AI works best as an execution layer around clear processes. If workflows are undefined, AI will scale inconsistency faster.
