Supplier discovery becomes slow and risky when data is scattered across inboxes and spreadsheets. A structured process improves speed and quality at the same time.

## Suggested workflow

1. Define category and risk requirements.
2. Source candidates from approved channels.
3. Run capability and compliance screening.
4. Request technical and commercial submissions.
5. Score suppliers with a shared matrix.
6. Store decisions and next actions in CRM.

## What to standardize

- Supplier profile fields.
- Evaluation scorecards.
- Approval steps and owners.
- Requalification review schedule.

A repeatable supplier discovery process reduces delays in production planning and lowers commercial risk.
