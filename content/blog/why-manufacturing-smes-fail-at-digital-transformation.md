Most digital transformation failures are not technology failures. They are sequencing failures.

## The top causes

- No defined business outcome.
- Too many systems changed at once.
- Poor data standards across teams.
- No adoption plan for day-to-day users.
- Success measured by launch, not by performance change.

## A better approach

- Start with one high-friction workflow.
- Define baseline metrics before implementation.
- Use phased rollouts and short feedback loops.
- Build dashboards leaders can act on weekly.

Digitalization should improve quote speed, reliability, and decision quality. If those outcomes are not measured, transformation becomes expensive activity without business impact.
