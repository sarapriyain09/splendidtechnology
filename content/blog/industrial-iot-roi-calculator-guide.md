Industrial IoT investments are easier to approve when teams use a clear ROI model. Start with operational losses you can measure now.

## Core ROI inputs

- Current unplanned downtime hours per month.
- Cost per downtime hour.
- Emergency maintenance and rush-parts spend.
- Planned reduction from monitoring and alerts.
- Deployment and ongoing platform cost.

## Simple annual ROI model

Annual savings = avoided downtime cost + avoided emergency spend.

Net benefit = annual savings - annual IoT program cost.

ROI percent = (net benefit / annual IoT program cost) x 100.

Use conservative assumptions for your first business case and validate with a pilot.
