Quote-to-order delay is one of the largest hidden revenue leaks in manufacturing sales. Workflow automation fixes handoff gaps that slow conversions.

## High-impact automation points

- Auto-assign new enquiries by product and region.
- Trigger technical review tasks with due dates.
- Generate quote checklists for consistency.
- Send timed follow-up reminders after quote issue.
- Flag stalled deals for manager intervention.

## What to monitor

- Time from enquiry to first quote.
- Quote revision frequency.
- Order conversion rate by segment.

Automation should reduce admin load while improving buyer response speed.
