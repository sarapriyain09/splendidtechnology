Manufacturing SEO works when positioning is specific and evidence-driven. Generic technology content rarely converts industrial buyers.

## Priority SEO moves

- Focus homepage intent around core commercial offer.
- Build manufacturing-specific service landing pages.
- Publish proof-led case studies with measurable outcomes.
- Add location pages for priority UK markets.
- Strengthen internal linking from blogs to service pages.

## Topic clusters to build

- Manufacturing CRM and lead management.
- Digitalization workflows for industrial teams.
- Condition monitoring and predictive maintenance.
- Reliability engineering and downtime reduction.

Consistency beats volume. Publish fewer high-intent articles tied directly to your services and case evidence.
