Strong case studies convert because they show before-and-after evidence, not feature lists.

## Recommended structure

1. Context: business model, team size, and sales process.
2. Problem: where opportunities were being lost.
3. Solution: workflow and system changes implemented.
4. Results: measured commercial impact.
5. Next phase: what scaled after initial rollout.

## Metrics to include

- First-response time.
- Quote turnaround time.
- Follow-up completion rate.
- Conversion rate by source.
- Pipeline forecast variance.

If you cannot measure the result, the case study will not build trust with decision-makers.
