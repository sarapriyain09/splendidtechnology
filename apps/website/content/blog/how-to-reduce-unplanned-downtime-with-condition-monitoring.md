Downtime reduction starts with focus. Monitor the assets that cause the biggest production impact when they fail.

## Step-by-step approach

1. Build a critical asset list.
2. Define failure indicators per asset type.
3. Set alert thresholds and escalation owners.
4. Run weekly reliability review meetings.
5. Track intervention lead time and incident trend.

## Metrics that prove progress

- Unplanned stoppage hours.
- Mean time between failures.
- Emergency maintenance spend.
- Percentage of planned vs reactive work.

Condition monitoring is most effective when alerts are tied to clear maintenance actions.
