Many manufacturing SMEs ask whether CRM or ERP should come first. The right answer depends on your immediate bottleneck.

## Choose CRM first if

- Leads are not tracked reliably.
- Quote follow-up is inconsistent.
- Sales forecasting is weak.

## Choose ERP first if

- Work orders and inventory are chaotic.
- Procurement delays are frequent.
- Production reporting lacks trust.

## Most SMEs should phase both

Phase 1: CRM for enquiry, quote, and pipeline control.

Phase 2: ERP-aligned workflows for operations and inventory.

Phase 3: Integrate commercial and operational data for end-to-end visibility.

This phased model gives faster ROI than a large all-at-once replacement program.
