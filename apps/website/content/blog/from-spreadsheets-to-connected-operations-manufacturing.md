Spreadsheets are useful until growth makes them risky. Version conflicts, manual reporting, and hidden delays become operational drag.

## Migration path that works

1. Map one high-friction workflow end to end.
2. Define required data fields and ownership.
3. Replace manual handoffs with workflow automation.
4. Add dashboards for weekly operational decisions.
5. Expand to adjacent processes in phases.

## Change management tips

- Keep process steps familiar for frontline users.
- Train by role with real scenarios.
- Measure adoption, not just system logins.

Connected operations are built through practical sequencing, not a single big-bang rollout.
