Automation fails when teams use inconsistent names, codes, and status definitions. Data standardization is the foundation of scalable digitalization.

## Where to standardize first

- Customer and account identifiers.
- Product and part classifications.
- Opportunity and job status values.
- Failure code taxonomy.
- Site and asset naming structure.

## Immediate benefits

- Faster reporting with fewer manual corrections.
- Cleaner integrations between CRM and operations systems.
- Better AI and analytics reliability.

Standardization is not a one-off cleanup. It needs ownership, rules, and periodic governance reviews.
