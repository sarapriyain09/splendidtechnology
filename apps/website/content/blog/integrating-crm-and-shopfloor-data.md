When CRM and shopfloor data are disconnected, sales teams overpromise and operations teams firefight. Integration improves planning and trust.

## Key integration outcomes

- Sales sees realistic production capacity signals.
- Operations sees demand pipeline changes earlier.
- Customers get clearer delivery expectations.
- Leadership gets one view of demand and execution.

## Data flows to prioritize

- Opportunity volume to planning teams.
- Capacity and lead-time updates to commercial teams.
- Order status and delay risk to account managers.

Integration does not need to be complex on day one. Start with two-way visibility for lead time and order status.
