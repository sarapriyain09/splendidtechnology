Most B2B lead loss in manufacturing happens between first enquiry and technical quotation. A clear CRM workflow closes that gap.

## Essential workflow stages

- New enquiry.
- Qualification.
- Technical review.
- Quote issued.
- Follow-up and negotiation.
- Won or lost with reason code.

## Rules that improve conversion

- Time-based SLAs for each stage.
- Named owner at every handoff.
- Automated reminders for overdue actions.
- Mandatory loss reasons for learning loops.

Reliable lead management is less about tools and more about execution discipline supported by CRM automation.
