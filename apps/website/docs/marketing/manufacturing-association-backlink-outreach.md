# Manufacturing Association Backlink Outreach Plan

Goal: acquire high-quality contextual backlinks from relevant UK manufacturing and engineering communities.

## Target association types

- UK manufacturing associations
- Engineering and reliability institutions
- Regional manufacturing clusters and chambers
- Industrial innovation networks
- Supply chain and exporter communities

## Outreach assets to prepare

- One-page company capability sheet (PDF)
- Two short case study summaries with outcomes
- One educational article link for each outreach segment
- Founder/technical lead profile and headshot

## Suggested outreach targets (examples to research and qualify)

- Make UK
- IET industry communities
- IMechE regional groups
- British Chambers of Commerce regional manufacturing groups
- Manufacturing Technology Centre partner network
- Local enterprise partnerships with manufacturing initiatives
- Regional manufacturing clusters in Midlands and North West
- Sector-specific associations tied to your strongest verticals

Note: confirm editorial rules and membership requirements before pitching.

## Link acquisition workflow

1. Build a prospect list with contact owner, website authority, and relevance score.
2. Send 15 to 20 tailored outreach emails per month.
3. Follow up at day 5 and day 12.
4. Track response rate, accepted placements, and acquired backlinks.
5. Refresh anchor text mix to avoid over-optimization.

## Anchor text policy

- 60% branded anchors (Splendid Technology, Splendid Technology UK)
- 25% partial-match anchors (CRM for manufacturers, Industrial IoT for UK SMEs)
- 15% generic anchors (learn more, this guide)

## Quality controls

- Prioritize relevance over raw domain metrics.
- Avoid paid link schemes and low-quality directories.
- Prefer editorial links from useful educational content.
- Maintain a monthly backlink quality review.
