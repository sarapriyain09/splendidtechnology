## Issue 2 - Phase 2: 2027-2028 Engineering Software Launch

Title:
Phase 2 - Engineering Platform Launch (Engineering AI + Motor Studio + Digital Twin)

Labels:
strategy, portfolio, business-2, engineering-platform, phase-2, priority-high

Assignees (suggested):
- Owner: Engineering Platform Lead
- Support: AI Platform Lead
- Support: Architecture Lead
- Support: Design Partner Program Lead

Body:

### Objective
Launch the Engineering Software business with a defensible studio model based on domain expertise and measurable engineering outcomes.

### Scope
- Establish Engineering Platform foundation services and contracts.
- Release Engineering AI and first two studios: Motor Studio and Digital Twin Studio.
- Run design-partner program with early paid pilots.

### Engineering and product checklist
- [ ] Define and approve ADR set for Engineering Platform architecture.
- [ ] Implement core contracts for simulation, twin, knowledge, and workflow orchestration.
- [ ] Ship Motor Studio MVP workflows for engineering design analysis.
- [ ] Ship Digital Twin Studio MVP workflows for scenario simulation and diagnostics.
- [ ] Add engineering copilot prompts and approval-aware execution patterns.
- [ ] Build knowledge ingestion and retrieval workflows for standards and lessons learned.
- [ ] Launch design-partner onboarding playbook with pilot instrumentation.
- [ ] Create value dashboard for engineering time savings and cycle-time reduction.

### Acceptance criteria
- [ ] At least initial paying design partners onboarded and active.
- [ ] Measurable engineering productivity gains documented in pilot outcomes.
- [ ] Cross-studio contracts support reuse without major rewrites.
- [ ] Engineering AI workflows demonstrate traceable decision support quality.

### Non-functional requirements
- [ ] End-to-end observability for simulation and twin execution pipelines.
- [ ] Versioned prompt and workflow definitions for reproducibility.
- [ ] Security controls validated for customer engineering data isolation.

### Dependencies
- Business 1 cash-flow and operating support.
- Shared platform services: auth, events, memory, monitoring.

### Risks
- Under-scoped data contracts may block studio reuse.
- Pilot outcomes may vary without strict value instrumentation.

### Definition of done
- [ ] Quarterly phase-gate confirms Business 2 launch readiness.
- [ ] Portfolio review approves controlled expansion toward hardware entry phase.
