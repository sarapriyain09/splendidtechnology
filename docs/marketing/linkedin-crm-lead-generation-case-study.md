# LinkedIn Repost Draft - CRM Lead Generation Case Study

## Hook
Most SMEs do not have a lead generation problem.
They have a lead management problem.

Leads are often scattered across spreadsheets, inboxes, and notes.
Follow-ups become inconsistent.
Decision-makers get missed.
Pipeline visibility disappears.

## The Challenge
We kept seeing the same issues:
- Limited contact information for newly registered companies
- Too much manual research to find decision-makers
- No clear tracking across multiple opportunities
- Inconsistent follow-up activity
- Poor visibility of pipeline movement

## The Solution
We built Splendid CRM with a configurable pipeline model designed for B2B outreach workflows.

### Lead Sources
New businesses:
- Company registration data
- Company websites
- Industry directories

Established businesses:
- Google Business profiles
- Industry databases
- Website research

Decision-maker identification:
- LinkedIn research
- Sales Navigator integration
- Contact enrichment

### Example Pipeline
Target Company -> Research Completed -> Decision Maker Identified -> LinkedIn Connection Sent -> Conversation Started -> Meeting Scheduled -> Proposal Submitted -> Won Opportunity

## What Worked Best
After testing multiple channels, LinkedIn became the most effective strategy.

Why:
- Cold calling required high effort
- Generic email had low response rates
- New companies often lacked accessible contact data

Winning approach:
1. Identify target companies
2. Find relevant decision-makers on LinkedIn
3. Build professional connections
4. Develop conversations
5. Convert into meetings and opportunities

## CRM Features Used
- Custom Pipeline Builder
- Lead Management
- Contact Management
- Activity Tracking
- Follow-up Reminders
- Opportunity Management
- Sales Dashboard
- Customer Notes
- Lead Source Tracking

## Result
This gave teams:
- A structured lead generation process
- Better visibility of sales activities
- Improved follow-up consistency
- Easier tracking of LinkedIn engagement
- Centralized customer information
- Measurable pipeline progress

## Key Takeaway
"Successful B2B lead generation is not about collecting thousands of contacts. It is about identifying the right decision-maker, building relationships, and managing every interaction through a structured sales process."
